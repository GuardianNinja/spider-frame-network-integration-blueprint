Mission Statement

Spider-Frame provides a FAAO-style identity and routing blueprint for secure cross-domain operations across GO, AO, and SO environments, while keeping policy decisions explicit and auditable.

System Overview

The system processes identity tokens in the format [SCALE]-[STATE]-[FULLNAME]-[DOMAIN]-[FACILITY]-[MISSION], resolves operational context, validates credentials, applies policy rules, and then executes approved service actions.

Orbit awareness (LEO, MEO, GEO) is integrated into context resolution, packet contracts, and policy hooks.

Architecture Layers

  • Identity parsing and context resolution
  • Security and credential verification (including compact credential tokens)
  • Policy engine with mission/domain/scale and optional orbit constraints
  • Packet contracts and integration transport schemas
  • Operational telemetry and failure handling
  • Cognitive Training System (CTS) for civilian, cadet, and industry readiness

FAAO Alignment

Spider-Frame mirrors FAAO-grade operational discipline with deterministic routing attributes, secure corridor enforcement, and closed-by-default authorization behavior for unsupported permissions.

Civilian + Industry Unification

The architecture unifies civilian learning, cadet development, and industry operator readiness using common credentialing logic, packet semantics, and telemetry-backed operational practices.
