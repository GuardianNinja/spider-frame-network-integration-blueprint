Cognitive Training Specification 1.0

Unified neuro-operational conditioning for civilian, cadet, and industry operator readiness.

OverviewPurposeCognitive PrinciplesTraining ArchitectureTraining FlowFAAO Alignment

Purpose

CTS builds repeatable neuro-conditioning rituals for memory, operational discipline, and multi-step authentication proficiency across public training pathways.

Cognitive Principles

  • Repetition reinforces procedural memory, pattern recognition, and sequence recall.
  • Complexity stimulates executive function, working memory, and decision flexibility.
  • Ritual creates cognitive anchoring and lower stress-load execution.

Training Architecture

Four layered progression model:

  • Layer 1: basic pattern recognition
  • Layer 2: multi-step authentication
  • Layer 3: operator-grade rituals
  • Layer 4: optional cognitive stress conditioning

Cadet Cognitive Ladder

Cadet development advances from sequence recall to mission-tier decision rituals with explicit timing and accuracy thresholds at each layer.

Training Flow

CTS phases: initiation, credential recall, sequence execution, cognitive reinforcement, and operator conditioning.

Integration points cover module contracts, packet schema, credential token literacy, telemetry capture, and failure-mode handling.

Spider-Frame Integration

CTS emits packetized readiness signals (CTS_MEMORY_CHALLENGE, CTS_SEQUENCE_VALIDATION, CTS_OPERATOR_READINESS) and telemetry for recall accuracy, sequence timing, and readiness scoring.

FAAO Alignment

CTS is the public training layer for FAAO-grade identity verification, mission access flow, multi-layer credentialing, and orbital discipline.

Expansion Path

Future extensions can add adaptive coaching models, richer stress scenarios, and mission-specific progression policies.
